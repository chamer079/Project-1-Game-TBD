How to Play

The player has 6 chances to correctly guess a 5 letter word. The squares will change color to represent one of the following scenarios:

1. The square will be grayed out if the letter is not in the word.

E

2. The square will turn yellow if the letter is in the word, but in the wrong space.

B

3. The square will turn green if the letter is in the word and in the correct space.

B
